Welcome to the COVID-19 Dashboard

Please click on the tabs to switch between the United States and World data.

Below you will find graphical repesentations of the number of COVID-19 reported tests, cases, hospitalizations, and deaths. By default you will see those statistics for the United States or Worldwide from March 05, 2020 to present day depending on which tab you select. You can refine the data by entering a specific state (United States tab) or country (Worldwide tab). You can further refine the data by either toggling one of the date range buttons or selecting a specific date range with the calendar.

The data available only covers March 2020 through present day.
